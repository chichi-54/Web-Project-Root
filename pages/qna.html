Student & Parent Q&A Forum

Browse questions from students and parents about career planning, AP classes, college readiness, internships, and more.

If you have a question, submit it below — judges can try asking questions too!

Parent: Do AP classes actually help with college admissions?

Yes. AP courses demonstrate academic rigor and readiness. Many colleges even offer credits for high AP exam scores.

Student: What internships can high schoolers apply for with no experience?

Many companies offer high‑school internships—especially in tech, healthcare, nonprofits, and government. Volunteer work also counts as experience.

Student: How early should I start preparing for college applications?

Most students begin in 10th grade by improving GPA, exploring interests, and building a resume. Application work usually starts in 11th grade.

Parent: What should my child include in their first resume?

Students should include volunteer work, school activities, awards, skills, leadership roles, and AP/dual‑credit courses.
