Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions high school students ask when exploring a career readiness program. If your question isn’t listed, feel free to visit our Q&A page and post it there!

What is the Career Readiness Learning Hub?

It’s an interactive platform designed to help students build essential skills, explore career paths, and prepare for college, jobs, and future opportunities using guided tools and resources.

Do I need to create an account to use the site?

No account is required to access most resources. However, signing in allows you to save your progress, track completed modules, and personalize your dashboard experience.

Are the resources free?

Yes! All tools, guides, and learning materials on this hub are completely free for educational use.

How can I improve my resume?

Check out our resume tips and templates under the resources section. You can also explore sample resumes and common mistakes to avoid.

How do I find out what career path might fit me?

Visit the Career Path resources page to explore different industries, required skills, and potential future opportunities based on your interests.

Where can I ask my own questions?

If your question isn’t listed here, head over to our Q&A page and submit a new question. Other students and mentors can respond with helpful feedback!

Still Need Help?

Visit the Q&A page to ask your own questions or browse answers submitted by other students.
